How You Changed Me

Before you, I was a story without a favourite chapter. Then you arrived.

You gave me peace

My mind was a storm with no harbour. You became the stillness after the rain, and for the first time, being quiet felt like enough.

You made me grow

Not by demanding I change, but by loving me so sincerely that I wanted to become better, not for you, but because you believed I could.

You brought warmth

I had become skilled at being fine. Then you arrived and reminded me what it truly feels like to be genuinely, softly happy.

You steadied my world

When everything tilted, you were the quiet constant. You did not fix everything. You simply stayed, and that was infinitely enough.

You made me braver

Knowing someone like you exists in my life makes everything feel more possible. You are my softest form of courage.

You became my home

Home is not always a place. Sometimes it is a person who makes every ordinary moment feel like exactly where you belong.

My love, I am not sure you fully understand the quiet revolution that happened inside me the day you decided to stay. You did not walk in with fanfare. You arrived softly, the way morning light does, slowly, warmly, inevitably. And before I could catch my breath, everything looked different. The colours were brighter. The silence was kinder. And I was, without entirely realising it, becoming someone I actually liked. That is entirely your fault, and I am completely unapologetic about every bit of it.

Thank You, My Love

For every small, significant, extraordinary thing you do without even realising how much it means

To my sweet therapist without a degree You never studied psychology. You simply understood me. That, darling, is a talent no university could ever teach.

For listening when my mind would not quiet down

At 2am when the thoughts arrive uninvited and my sentences stop making sense halfway through, you stayed. You listened not to respond but to understand. That is a rare and precious thing.

For staying during the difficult chapters

When I was not easy to love, heavy and complicated and too quiet for too long, you did not disappear. You stayed not because you had to. Because you wanted to. That is the kind of love songs are written about.

For your patience, which is honestly a superpower

I take my time. I overthink. I circle back to things three conversations later. And yet there you are, patient as a Sunday morning, never rushing me, never making me feel like too much.

For being my comfort person

There is a certain relief that comes only from reaching for someone specific. You are that person for me. On the hardest days, the softest days, and all the ordinary ones in between, you are who I reach for.

For choosing me quietly, every single day

Love is not one grand declaration. It is a thousand small choices made daily. Every time you showed up, every time you stayed, you were choosing me. That is the most beautiful thing anyone has ever done.
